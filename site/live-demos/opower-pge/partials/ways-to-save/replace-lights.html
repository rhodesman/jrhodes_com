PG&E Lighting Rebate available

Download

Because lighting systems can be expensive to update, PG&E offers two kinds of rebates to help you defray costs:

1. Replacement Lamp Incentives.
Earn instant rebates from participating lighting distributors when you purchase and install qualifying replacement lamps.

2. Replacement Fixture And Retrofit Kit Incentives..
These rebates are designed to help cover the installation costs of qualifying fixtures or kits. Our Lighting Rebate Catalog has details on how to take advantage of these rebates. You can also apply and check the status of your rebate online through our eRebates program.
