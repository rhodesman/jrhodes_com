This Week, George is Curious About...

Birthdays!

What do birthdays mean to you? Birthdays mean different things to different families. Encourage your students to share what a birthday means to them and what their families do to celebrate.

Have the students write their birthdays on a cutout of a birthday balloon and then sit in a circle in order of the dates. Go around the room and have each student share what a birthday means to them and how their family celebrates a birthday. Have the students explain why they think birthdays are fun and what they like to receive.

About Curious George

Curious George by H.
