What is CuriousWorld.com? Back to Top ∧

  • What is CuriousWorld.com?

    CuriousWorld.com informs parents about developmental milestones for kids, ages 3-7. In total, there are approximately 200 milestones that correspond to school readiness. CuriousWorld.com lets parents and kids practice these skills and track a child's growth across 8 key learning areas.

Cards Back to Top ∧

These are micro-activities for anytime, anywhere learning. Try out the free cards to test the features of our site. Purchase the entire set and you will get full coverage of all the skills your child needs for balanced growth and learning.

  • What’s the best way to use the Cards?

    We believe exploration of all domains leads to balanced learning, so we encourage you to sample cards from all domains.

  • How long will I have access to them?

    You will be able to access your cards for one year from the date of purchase.

  • What is the Cardfolio?

    A digital scrapbook for tracking your child’s progress. Parents can attach visual proof, such as photos and videos, to demonstrate completion of a card.

  • Will other users be able to see my Cardfolio content?

    No, only you can see the content contained within your Cardfolio.

  • I have questions regarding the privacy of my content. Who can I contact?

    Please contact privacy@hmhco.com with any questions regarding privacy.

Store Back to Top ∧

Gift boxes are curated collections of new and timeless classics. The book sets are aligned to eight key developmental areas and help target skills across a broad range of academic and social topics.

  • Can I purchase Gift Boxes outside the United States?

    Sorry. We only ship products within the United States.

  • Can I replace one book in a gift box with one from a different set?

    You cannot request replacements at this time.

  • Can I return my gift box?

    Yes. To receive a full refund, we require that:

    • Credit card orders must be returned within 30 days of receipt
    • Attach our label to your box for quick refund
    • All items in the gift box be returned
    • Items must be in new condition
    • Items must be returned with all original packaging and accessories
    • Media items (for example, movies, music, video games and software) must be unopened and still in their plastic wrap

    Click Here to print the label and paperwork needed to make your return.
    Note: Return shipping may be deducted from the amount of your refund if the reason for your return is not due to HMH error.
