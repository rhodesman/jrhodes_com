About Curious George

Curious George by Margret and H. A. Rey was first published by Houghton Mifflin in 1941, and since then books about the mischievous monkey have been capturing the hearts and minds of readers throughout the world. Creators Margret and H. A. Rey wrote seven original tales about Curious George, and he has become one of the most recognizable and beloved children's book characters.
