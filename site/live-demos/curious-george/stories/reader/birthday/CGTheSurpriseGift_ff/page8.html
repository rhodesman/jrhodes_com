George realized

there was a lot of food in

the kitchen that could be

unwrapped! Bananas, apples,

cheese, even an onion — stinky!

Soon George had unwrapped many

yummy things. Maybe too many . . .

But he still didn’t know what was under the wrapping

paper of the gift.

Before George could let his curiosity get the best of him, his friend sent him

to the department store to pick up his new suit.
