After lunch the Pig Scouts decided to go for a hike. Off

they went, with Pinkerton leading the line, of course. As

the Pig Scouts marched across the sand, they heard a

faint voice far in the distance.

The voice called out, "Who would care for a sandwich?"

Pinkerton pricked up his pointy ears.

Care for a sandwich?

Oh yes, me first! he thought,

and he began to trot ahead of the others.
