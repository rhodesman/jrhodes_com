Now at a full gallop Pinkerton shrieked,

"ME FIRST!"

Over a sandy hill he flew and . . .

Kerplop.

He landed face to face with a small creature

with a bump on her nose and fur on her toes.

"Am I glad to see you! " she cackled.

"I sure could hear you coming:

'Me first. ME FIRST! ME FIRST!'

I guess you really would care for a sandwich."
