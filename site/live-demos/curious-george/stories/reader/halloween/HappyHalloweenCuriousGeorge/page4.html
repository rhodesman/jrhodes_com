Decorate the house up right,

Perfect for a spooky night.

Times for lots of Halloween fun—

Celebrating has begun!

Jack-o-lanterns—

scary faces!

Ghosts and goblins, take your places!

Cobwebs over every door.

Surely tricks and treats

in store.

Now it's time to

wait and see

Just what Curious

George will be!
