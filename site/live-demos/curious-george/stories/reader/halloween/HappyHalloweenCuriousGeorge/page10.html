Trick or treat! Costumes galore.

Why, George, just look who's at the door!

One teeny, tiny ghost

in white

Two grinning pirates—

what a fright!

Three witches

wearing pointed hats

Four mummy friends

and five black bats!

A bowl of treats

sits waiting there,

And George has

just the plan.

He hands them out to all his friends

As only monkeys can!
