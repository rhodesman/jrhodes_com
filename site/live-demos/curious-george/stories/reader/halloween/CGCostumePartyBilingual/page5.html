“Don’t be afraid, George,” said the man with the yellow hat. “This witch is

our friend.”

The witch took off her mask. It was Mrs. Gray after all! “Oh dear,” she

said. “Did I forget to tell you this was a costume party?”

—No tengas miedo, Jorge — dijo el señor del sombrero amarillo —. Esta

bruja es nuestra amiga.

La bruja se sacó la máscara. ¡Era la señora Gray!

— Oh, cariño — dijo —. ¿No te avisé que era una fiesta de disfraces?
