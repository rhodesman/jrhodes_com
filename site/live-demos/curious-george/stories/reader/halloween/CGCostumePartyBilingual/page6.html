George had never been to a costume party before. Inside he saw more

people that he knew. They were all wearing costumes. There was his

friend Betsy dressed up like an astronaut. And was that Bill? Why, he

looked just like a mummy!

Jorge nunca había estado en una fiesta de disfraces. Adentro vio más

gente conocida. Todos tenían disfraces. Su amiga Betsy estaba vestida de

astronauta. ¿Y ése era Bill? ¡Caramba, parecía una momia!

George wanted to wear a costume, too.

Jorge también quería ponerse un disfraz.

“I have some dress-up clothes upstairs,” said Mrs. Gray. “Would you like to

use them to make a costume, George?”

—Arriba tengo algunos disfraces —le dijo la señora Gray—. ¿Quieres eligir

uno para disfrazarte, Jorge?
