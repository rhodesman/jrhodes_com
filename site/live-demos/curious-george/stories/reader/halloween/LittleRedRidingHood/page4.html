One day her mother said to her,“Come here,

Little Red Riding Hood! Take this cake and

bottle of wine to Grandmother. She is weak and ill,

and they will do her good.

Go quickly before it gets too hot.

Don’t loiter along the way, nor run, or you will fall

and break the bottle, and there will be no wine for

Grandmother. When you get there, don’t forget

to say ‘Good morning’ prettily, without staring about you.“

“I will do just as you tell me,”

Little Red Riding Hood promised her mother.
