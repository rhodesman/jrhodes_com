Little Red Riding Hood picked flowers till she could

carry no more, and then she remembered her

grandmother again. She was astonished when she

got to the house to find the door open, and when

she entered the room everything seemed strange.

She felt quite frightened but she did not know why.

“Generally I like coming to see Grandmother so much,”

she thought.

“Good morning, Grandmother,” she cried.

But she received no answer.
