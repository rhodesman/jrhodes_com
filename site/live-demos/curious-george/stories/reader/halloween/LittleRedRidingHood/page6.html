Her grandmother lived away in the wood,

a good half hour from the village.

When she got to the wood she met a wolf,

but Little Red Riding Hood did not know what a wicked

animal he was, so she was not a bit afraid of him.

“Good morning, Little Red Riding Hood,” he said.

“Good morning, wolf,” she answered.

“Whither away so early, Little Red Riding Hood?”

“To Grandmother’s.”

“What have you got in your basket?”

“Cake and wine. We baked yesterday, so I am taking

a cake to her. She wants something to make her well.”

“Where does your grandmother live, Little Red Riding Hood?”

“A good quarter of an hour farther into the wood.

Her house stands under three big oak trees near a

hedge of nut trees which you must know,”

said Little Red Riding Hood.
