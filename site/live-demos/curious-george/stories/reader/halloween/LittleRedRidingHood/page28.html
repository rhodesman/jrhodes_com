So he pulled the sleeping wolf’s mouth wide open. The first thing

he saw was the little red cloak. The little girl sprang out and

cried,

“Oh, how frightened I was!

It was so dark inside the wolf.” Next the old grandmother came,

alive but hardly able to breathe.

The wolf awoke and tried to spring up,

but he was so frightened

by the sight of the hunter

that he fell down dead.
