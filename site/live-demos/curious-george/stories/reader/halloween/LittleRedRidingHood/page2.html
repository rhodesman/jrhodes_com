nce

upon a time

there was a sweet little maiden who

was loved by all who knew her.

She was especially dear to her

grandmother, who did not know

how to make enough of the child.

Once she gave her a little red velvet cloak.

It was so becoming and she liked it so much

that she would never wear anything else.

So she got the name of Little Red Riding Hood.
